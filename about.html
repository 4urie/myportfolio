About Me

Turning academic projects into real, working systems.

I'm Nash Napuli, a 4th-year BSIT student at Davao del Norte State College with hands-on experience building web applications, mobile apps, and simulation systems. I care about writing code that's clean, purposeful, and maintainable. Whether integrating REST APIs, structuring a Flutter app, or rendering 3D environments in the browser, I focus on making things that actually work well not just look good on paper.

Currently in my final year and open to internship opportunities, freelance projects, or collaborative work in web and mobile development. If you're building something that needs a developer who takes both the code and the craft seriously let's talk.
